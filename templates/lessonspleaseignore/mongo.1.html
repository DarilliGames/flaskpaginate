First off lets get your Mongo URI

Click on Clusters - My database is called "testerMongo", yours may be different. Then Click Connect.

You will then be given options on how you want to connect. Select the option "Connect Your Applications".
The comment for this should be "Get a connection string and view driver connection examples".
You will be greeted by this pop up. Select your Language (Python) and then a Python Version (3.6) and you will be given a connection String.
